## 修复

```xml
<style name="AppTheme" parent="Theme.AppCompat.DayNight.NoActionBar">
    <item name="android:colorMode">wideColorGamut</item>
</style>
```

需 `COLOR_MODE_WIDE_COLOR_GAMUT` + `PixelFormat.RGBA_F16`
